Everybody loves Chocolate, but not everybody knows, where it is originated from.

This irresistible treat comes from a cacao tree, a tropical plant that grows only within 20 degrees’ latitude of the Equator. The main cacao-producing countries are the Ivory Coast and Ghana, but you find a lot of other smaller markets in South America and Africa. The cacao tree doesn’t prefer certain seasons, its produces pods all year long, so it will throw pods in every stage of ripeness, from the very early flowering bud to the most ripe and mature pod ready to be harvest.
