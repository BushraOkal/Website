The ancient Mesoamericans

where the first who came in contact with thecacao plants that was found in the
tropical rainforests of Central America. They roasted, fermented and ground ,the cacao beans into a paste.
Other ingredients were water, vanilla, honey, chili peppers and spices to produce the chocolate drink.

Chocolate made the way to Spain.

Because it was a very demanded and luxury product,
the Spanish would prefer chocolate over silver and gold. Although they sweetened the bitter
drink with cane sugar and cinnamon. The demand for chocolate grew and it became a symbol of luxury,
wealth and power. To be able to cover the demand, European powers established colonial plantations in
regions around the world to grow cacao and sugar.

The first cocoa press machine

was created from the Dutch chemist Coenraad
Johannes van Houten in 1828. It could squeeze the fatty cocoa butter from roasted cacao beans,
leaving behind a dry cake that could be pulverized into a fine powder.
The first solid chocolate bar was created from 1847 form the British chocolate company,
J.S. Fry & Sons created. Today, Lindt chocolate is known to be one of the best Chocolates available.
The inventor Name was Rodolph Lindt, who built the conching machine in 1879, which produced in large amounts with the help of factory assembly lines.
